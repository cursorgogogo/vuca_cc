VUCA Leadership Assessment

Evaluate your leadership style and identify how well you adapt to volatile, uncertain, complex, and ambiguous environments.

Assessment Questions

Please rate each statement based on how well it describes your typical leadership behavior. Be honest with your responses for the most accurate results.

Volatility

I adapt my leadership approach quickly when circumstances change unexpectedly.

I maintain a clear vision that helps my team navigate through rapid changes.

I make decisions quickly even when information is incomplete.

Uncertainty

I actively seek diverse perspectives and information before making important decisions.

I invest time in learning and building understanding of complex situations.

I acknowledge uncertainty openly and help my team navigate it effectively.

Complexity

I simplify complex information to help my team understand and act effectively.

I consider how different parts of the system interact when making decisions.

I build cross-functional teams to address complex challenges.

Ambiguity

I experiment with different approaches when the best path forward is unclear.

I maintain flexibility in my plans and can pivot when needed.

I learn from failures and use them to improve future decisions.
